Welcome...

Namastey, My journey started from Tezpur (the place where I was born and graduated in the year 2003). Moved to Guwahati for my Masters (in Physics, 2005). Shifted my base to Pune in 2008, in pursuit of the Degree of Philosophy in Atmospheric and Space Sciences (2014). I came to Victoria in June, 2014 as a post-doctoral Fellow to work on the simulation of the Indian summer monsoon using the Multi-cloud-model convective parameterization approach with Prof. Boualem Khouider in the department of Math and Stats, UVic.

Research Interests:

  • Indian Summer Monsoon
  • Convective Parameterization
  • Extremes
